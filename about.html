Our story

A local mother-daughter duo building something beautiful together

Anaya Jewelry is rooted in family. We’re a local mother-daughter duo creating pieces that feel warm, personal, and easy to treasure.

We want the shop to feel warm, cheerful, and easy to browse — the kind of little online store that feels personal from the first click.

What matters to us

Sweet presentation, simple shopping

We want every visitor to understand what they’re buying quickly — from materials and size to pricing and shipping — while still feeling the warmth of a real family business.

The goal is simple: beautiful pieces, straightforward product pages, and a sweet little-shop experience that works especially well on mobile.

What shoppers can expect

  • Clean menus and easy browsing
  • Simple product categories and filtering
  • Multiple product images when available
  • Visible pricing, shipping, and product specs
