Our story

A local mother-daughter duo building something beautiful together

Anaya Jewelry is rooted in family. We’re a local mother-daughter duo creating pieces that feel warm, personal, and easy to treasure.

What we love most is the human side of it — sharing pieces we genuinely find beautiful, offering a more personal shopping experience, and creating a brand that feels inviting from the first click to the final package.

What matters to us

Thoughtful pieces, presented with care

We want Anaya to feel welcoming. That means elegant jewelry, honest presentation, and a sense that there are real people behind the brand.

We’re not trying to be the biggest shop on the internet. We’d rather be the one that feels personal, kind, and memorable.

Why people connect with us

  • Local, family-run feel
  • Small-batch collection with personality
  • Warm and inviting presentation
  • Pieces chosen to be easy to wear and easy to gift
